# Initial Concept
A precision timezone planner for seamless global team coordination.

# Product Definition

## Target Users
- **Distributed Teams and Remote Workers:** Primary focus on professionals coordinating across multiple geographies and timezones.

## Goals & Objectives
- **Minimize Cognitive Friction:** Eliminate the "mental math" typically required for cross-timezone scheduling through intuitive localized interfaces.
- **High-Fidelity Dashboard:** Provide a sophisticated visual representation of real-time availability across the entire team.
- **Respect Work-Life Balance:** Empower teams to find meeting windows that respect local business hours and personal boundaries.

## Visual Identity & Brand Tone
- **Architectural Precision:** A professional, dark-mode-first aesthetic characterized by clean lines, technical typography (JetBrains Mono), and high-fidelity Glassmorphism.

## Priority Features
- **DST Intelligence:** Proactive warnings and impact analysis for Daylight Savings Time transitions to prevent scheduling errors.
- **Localized Context:** Individual time scales for every city and a multi-timezone scrubber for instantaneous local time lookups.
- **Smart Slot Analysis:** Advanced algorithms to identify the "Golden Hour" for meetings or provide home-base priority fallbacks.
